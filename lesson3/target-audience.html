Target Audience

  • Entrepeneurs looking for support as they consider starting a new business.
  • Businesses that want more opportunities to engage with their local communities.
  • Families, schools, clubs, and other organizations looking to request local support for community projects.
  • Local business owners with strong experience running and managing employees who also want to help others become successful.

    Steve Howard

    Successful business owner

    Steve volunteers his time and business resources to help new businesses with tough issues like hiring stragies and employee engagement. He feels its his responsibility to give back to a community that has given him so much.

    Hugh Sanders

    Coorporate raider turned non profit grant writer.

    Hugh spent several years as a financial analyst for a large coorporation that made money by purchasing undervalued assets and selling off pieces of them. He now sleeps better because he works for a local non-profit writing grants to benefit local at risk youth. He regularly reaches out to local businesses for supplies and donations to help with his events.

    Jane Thompson

    Entrepeneur thinking about opening her own steak restaurant.

    Jane has worked in many kitchens and has received many rewards. Her dream is to open her own place where she can only serve steak as its intended, medium rare. Jane knows her way around a grill but could use some support as she navigates building leases, investor financing, and dealing with customer complaints.
